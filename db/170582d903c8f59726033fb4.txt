<h2><a id="content-环境" href="https://blog.lsy223622.com/archives/14/#content-%E7%8E%AF%E5%A2%83" class="heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink"></a>环境</h2>
<p>需要以下环境运行</p>
<ul>
<li>Typecho 1.2+</li>
<li>PHP 7.2+</li>
<li>MySQL 5.7+</li>
</ul>
<h2><a id="content-安装" href="https://blog.lsy223622.com/archives/14/#content-%E5%AE%89%E8%A3%85" class="heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink"></a>安装</h2>
<ul>
<li>下载主题压缩包 <a rel="noopener noreferrer" href="https://github.com/liaocp666/Jasmine/releases/latest/download/jasmine.zip" title="jasmine.zip">jasmine.zip</a>
</li>
<li>解压下载文件，并上传至 <code>usr/themes/</code> 目录下</li>
<li>启用此主题</li>
</ul>
<h2><a id="content-更新" href="https://blog.lsy223622.com/archives/14/#content-%E6%9B%B4%E6%96%B0" class="heading-permalink" aria-hidden="true" title="Permalink"></a>更新</h2>
<ul>
<li>在主题选项中，点击创建备份，可备份现有主题选项**（可选）**</li>
<li>参考安装</li>
</ul>
